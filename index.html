Our Mission

BurrstSports helps athletes monetize their content in the form of NFTs. We are helping athletes take advantage of their NIL rights by partnering up with us to release NFTs into the digital world. An athlete’s NIL is important as they continue in their career and our creativity helps them separate from the pack. We understand the hard work athletes put in and believe they should be able to promote themselves with compensation in return. Building digital assets one athlete at a time.

Manifesto

We love playing & watching sports.

Sports allow us to bond.
Sports allow for new relationships to form.
Sports spark up conversations.
Whether you are at a basketball game in Chicago or Australia it doesn’t matter.
You can relate with people at the game.
Games are streamed from all over the world.
The digital era allows you to watch a game at your fingertips.
That is power.

Athletes do not have control over their NIL

For decades high school and college athletes would have to abide by rules and laws on how, when, and what they can promote of themselves.
They could never be compensated for their name, image, or likeness.
Every single day an athlete would make a sacrifice to sharpen and perfect their game.
It was a choice every athlete made without any compensation.
Focus, grit, discipline, courage and determination to get to the next level. At the professional level they would be paid based on their value.
But why not sooner?

BurrstSports is here to change that

We believe every athlete should be able to promote themselves and be compensated.
They shouldn’t have to wait until being a professional athlete because what about the athletes who didn’t make it?
The sacrifice to put in all that work and be cut short.
We are here to encourage athletes to still play and work hard but to be able to create digital assets that bring in profit.
We are here to create Digital Assets that allow fans to show financial support for their favorite athletes and to allow athletes to give back to their community.
We are here to design NFTs with athletes that show the world they are more than just an athlete.
Made for athletes. Designed by athletes. The time is now.
